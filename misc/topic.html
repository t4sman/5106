Our Topic


We want to take a closer look at the positive and negative impacts of the advancing technology and what this means for society. Specifically, we want to explore what kind of advancements there are to be made and how this technology will change our lives. We also want to look at what kind of advancements society had to go through to get us to this point. We want to research this topic because it spikes our interest about electronics and technology, and give us an opportunity to take a deeper look at how the world around us works. This topic feels important to us as these developments in technology will define what our lives will look like 10 years from now. And with such a foothold electronics have in our society now, We find it important to understand how much relief electronics give us in our daily lives.
